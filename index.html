The Global Entrepreneurship Exchange (GE2)

The Global Entrepreneurship Exchange (GE2) Workshop provides entrepreneurship education with a unique global experience, and applies concepts to the real world. The workshop is designed to explore the importance of entrepreneurship and innovation in today's global economy, and to cultivate an entrepreneurial mind-set. As part of this, students work in multi-disciplinary, multi-cultural teams to develop their ideas into vetted business concepts The workshop is organized by the Manning school of business, university of Massachusetts, Lowell, USA.
